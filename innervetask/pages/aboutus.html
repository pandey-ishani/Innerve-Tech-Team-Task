ABOUT US

In India, there are a number of students we find a number of bright students in the classrooms. But we can afford to overlook the students who are out of this lot just because they are either slow or the school environment is not getting them the personal attention they require to grow. Tutor's Hub was started to help students find the tutors near their houses who can give them personal attention and in some cases in a homely environment. There are a number of skilled people around us. As they say, knowledge grows more when you impart, we believe in connecting these people to the students who need assistance. Our aim is to provide students help from all the skilled people around them and at the same time offer employment to the person who teaches. This dual contribution to the society has made a leading startup in just two years of inception.
